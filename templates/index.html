WHY CHOOSE US

BUENO INDUSTRIES LIMITED

At Bueno Industries Limited, we ensure that our products meets International Standards, as we are fully equipped to effectively deliver value and exceed expectations.

The company was incorporated to provide quality service packages for Nigerians, foreign individuals and investors in the listed sectors of the economy.

Bueno Industries is the owner of BUENO brand of LPG Regulators and is currently working in close partnership with an Indian manufacturers of LPG Regulator products for the manufacturing/importation of BUENO brand of LPG Regulator and products into Nigeria, with future plan of developing local manufacturing facility for LPG regulators and other related products in a bid to provide competitive pricing for the products in the country..
